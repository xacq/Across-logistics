When to use air freight

Air freight is best suited for urgent, high-value or operationally critical cargo.

This service is recommended when delivery speed has a direct impact on production, commercial commitments, healthcare operations, replacement parts, time-sensitive documentation or customer delivery expectations.

Time-sensitive shipments

For cargo that must move quickly and cannot wait for slower transport modes.

Critical supply chain needs

For urgent components, stock replenishment, samples, spare parts or production support.

Regulated or special cargo

For shipments requiring specific documentation, safety procedures or handling standards.
